Your courses — your way


Course pages are designed to meet the needs of instructors. Students struggle to find content, find deadlines, or receive notifications. But it doesn't need to be this way. Students take their education into their hands everday, using services like Discord or Slack to collaborate. We want to give them the tools to do more.

With DTC-05's project, you can build course pages your own way. Create your own categories, put content where you want to find it, collaborate with other students to stay up to date — and more.

With DTC-05, your courses can be your own.
